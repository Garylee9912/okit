Ramen is Ramne

Welcome to Umami Ramen House, where authentic flavors and soul-warming bowls of ramen await. Our cozy corner in the city is a hidden gem for ramen lovers seeking an unforgettable culinary experience. Immerse yourself in the warm and inviting atmosphere as tantalizing aromas of simmering broths and sizzling toppings fill the air. From rich and savory tonkotsu to delicate and fragrant shoyu, our ramen bowls are crafted with precision and passion. Discover the true essence of Japanese comfort food at Umami Ramen House, where every bite takes you on a flavorful journey.

Recommendation: Try their famous Ramen!

Address: 434-1 Tsurugaoka, Tsurugashima, Saitama 350-2204

Sushi is Sushi

Welcome to Sakura Sushi Bar, a hidden gem where talented sushi chefs meticulously craft edible works of art. Delight in vibrant sashimi, expertly rolled creations, and tender premium-grade fish that will transport your taste buds to new heights. Immerse yourself in the elegant ambiance, complemented by impeccable service and a thoughtfully curated beverage selection. Experience a culinary journey that embodies the essence of Japan, where every visit to Sakura Sushi Bar is an unforgettable celebration of flavors and artistry.

Recommendation: Try their signature dish!

Address: 43-1 Kujirai Shinden, Kawagoe, Saitama 350-0809
